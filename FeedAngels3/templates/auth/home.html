Feed the Hungry Homeless People

Food waste has increased significantly. According to the Food and Agriculture Organization, 1/3 of all food produced for human use is thrown away, amounting to over 1.3 billion tons each year. On the other hand, according to the WHO, 20% of the population is facing severe food shortages. As a result, a solution that avoids food waste while also feeding the hungry is required.
